SAYOMBHU MUKDEEPROM

Best known for his work on the films of Apichatpong Weerasethakul, Mukdeeprom has been spreading his wings internationally, shooting such acclaimed films as Miguel Gomes’ Arabian Nights and the two recent features of Luca Guadagnino, Suspiria and Call Me By Your Name, for which he won back to back Best Cinematography Awards at the Independent Spirit Awards in 2017 and 2018. This Spotlight will focus on Mukdeeprom’s recent foray into international films and his experiences adjusting from working in Thailand to working all over the world.

Purin Pictures is a non-profit film fund that supports independent cinema in Southeast Asia. Started in 2017 under the Purin Foundation, our funding programs cover film production, film post-production, and film-related activities. To date, the films we have supported have found success at international festivals including Cannes, Venice, and Berlin, and helped bring Southeast Asian cinema to a wider international audience.
